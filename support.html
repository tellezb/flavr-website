How to Get Faster Help

  • Describe what happened and what you expected.
  • Include your device model and iOS/Android version.
  • Add screenshots or screen recordings when possible.
  • Tell us your app version and when the issue started.

What We Can Help With

  • Recipe sharing, saving, and organization problems.
  • Bug reports and performance troubleshooting.
  • Privacy and data requests.
